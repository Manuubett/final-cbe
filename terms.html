What Are Cookies & Local Storage?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. The CBE Mark Sheet System uses a combination of browser cookies and localStorage (a similar web storage mechanism) to remember your preferences and keep you signed in.

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking cookies. Our use of browser storage is strictly functional.

SECTION 22

Managing & Clearing Storage

You can clear all localStorage and sessionStorage data at any time through your browser settings:

  • Chrome / Edge: Settings → Privacy & Security → Clear browsing data → Cookies and other site data.
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data.
  • Safari (iOS/macOS): Settings → Safari → Advanced → Website Data → Remove All.

Note that clearing this data will sign you out and remove any cached preferences. It will not affect your school data stored in Firebase — all student records and results are safe in the cloud.

Because all storage items are strictly necessary for the System to function, we do not provide a cookie opt-out banner. Blocking these items may prevent the System from working correctly.
