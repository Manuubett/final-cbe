Our Story

From a classroom problem
to a national solution

The CBE Mark Sheet System was born from a very real frustration. When Kenya introduced the CBC curriculum, teachers across the country struggled to adapt their mark-keeping to the new assessment model. The eight-tier performance level system — from EE1 down to BE2 — meant that a simple spreadsheet was no longer enough.

In 2025, Bett KE Tech Ltd set out to build a system that understood the Kenyan classroom from the inside. Not a generic school management system retrofitted for Kenya — but one designed from scratch around the CBC curriculum, the Kenyan school calendar, M-Pesa payments, and the real constraints of school ICT budgets.

Today, the CBE Mark Sheet System supports Grades 1 through 9, covering all CBC subject sets for Lower Primary, Upper Primary, and Junior Secondary School. It handles compound subjects like English (Language + Composition) and Kiswahili (Lugha + Insha), auto-calculates overall averages, generates class rankings, and produces branded report cards — all in a browser, with no software to install.

We are based in Kenya and we build for Kenya. Every feature, every design decision, every pricing choice is made with the Kenyan teacher and school administrator in mind.
