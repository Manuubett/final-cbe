How it works
Simple by design
Up and running in minutes

No training needed. No Excel formulas. No printing headaches. Just your school's data, always organised and one click from a beautiful report.

1
Register your school
The school admin creates an account, sets up the school name, county, principal details and uploads the school logo. Takes under 2 minutes. Pay with M-Pesa — no bank account required.
Admin · One-time setup
2
Add students and teachers
Admit students into grades and streams. Add teachers manually or share a registration link — each teacher is linked to their class automatically and requires admin approval before going live.
Admin · Per term
3
Teachers enter marks from any device
Teachers open the mark sheet on phone or computer, type scores and CBC levels calculate instantly — EE1 through BE2, fully automatic. Autosave protects every entry even when the connection drops mid-session.
Teachers · Every term
4
Print branded reports instantly
Admin opens Analysis, selects any class and prints individual student reports or a full class ranking — all carrying the school logo, name and motto automatically. No extra setup per term.
Admin · End of term
What you get
Everything your school needs

A complete CBC management platform — from student admission to end-of-term reports, all in one place.

⚡
Instant CBC Level Calculation
Type a mark and EE, ME, AE, BE levels appear automatically. No formulas. No errors. The full 8-tier CBC grading scale is built in for all grades 1–9.
Live calculation
🖨️
Branded Print Reports
School name, logo and motto appear on every report card and mark sheet. Set once in Settings — applied everywhere, every term, automatically.
Auto branding
📈
Class Analysis Dashboard
Subject averages, level distribution charts, top performers and student rankings — generated instantly from saved marks. No extra work required.
One-click analysis
☁️
Real-Time Cloud Sync
Marks saved by a teacher are immediately visible to the admin. No USB drives, no email files, no version conflicts between devices anywhere.
Firebase powered
🔐
Role-Based Access
Teachers see only their class. Admins see everything. No teacher can access another class's data. Approval required before any account goes live.
Secure by design
📱
Works on Any Device
Optimised for phones, tablets and laptops. Number pad keyboard on mobile. Autosave keeps data safe even when connection drops mid-entry.
Mobile-first
